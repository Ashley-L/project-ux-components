// SPIDERMAN

"When you can do the things that I can, but you don't, and then the bad things happen, they happen because of you."

Bio

Peter gained his abilities after being bitten by a spider and decided to use his powers for good. Designing his own suit, web fluid, and web-shooters, he began to fight crime in his hometown of Queens, New York. He caught the attention of Tony Stark after videos of his feats were posted online, and ended up going to Germany to fight against Captain America and those against the Sokovia Accords with Tony and his supporters. After this civil war, he returned home and continued to protect his city in the hopes of proving himself worthy of joining the Avengers.

Despite being a teenager, Peter is committed to doing what he can to prevent harm from coming to the people in his city.

// IRON MAN

Tragically, this character is not in this series of movies.

“You can take away my house, all my tricks and toys. One thing you can’t take away… I am Iron Man.”

Bio

Iron Man is born in a cave, with a box of scraps, after Tony Stark is injured in a terrorist attack by the Ten Rings and taken prisoner in Afghanistan. With fellow prisoner Ho Yinsen, they build an arc reactor to both keep shrapnel out of his heart and power a mechanized suit he uses to escape. Further developing the suit into a weapon, he eventually takes on the mantle 'Iron Man' and becomes a cornerstone to S.H.I.E.L.D.'s Avengers Initiative.

Ethos

Though he was once accused of not ever being the one to make sacrifices, Tony has proven throughout the movies that he’s willing to fight for the greater good despite a heavy personal cost.

// KILLMONGER

Tragically, this character is not in this series of movies.

"People die every day. That's just part of life around here."

Bio

Erik Stevens learned he was from Wakanda, a rich but isolated African society, when his father was killed. His father had been working with a smuggler interested in Vibranium, of which Wakanda had plenty, and Stevens saw his father's death as an attempt to halt the sharing of resources with black people around the world. With intense training in the military, earning the name 'Killmonger' because of his kill count, Stevens prepared his entire life to take on the Black Panther mantle when he arrived Wakanda.

// LOKI

Tragically, this character is not in this series of movies.

"I am the monster parents tell their children about at night."

Bio

Burdened with glorious purpose, Loki grew up in Asgard as the son of Allfather Odin and the brother of Thor. Only when he was adult did he learn of his true heritage: he was actually the son of Laufey, a Frost Giant, and raised to be a bridge between the two races. This realization set Loki off on a destructive path to prove his worth, which involves trying to kill the Frost Giants and take over planet Earth.

Ethos

Loki plays by his own rules. He severs ties with allies as quickly as he creates them, and his own interests are his ultimate priority.
